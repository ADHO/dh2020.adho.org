1. Abstract

The presentation of the DH Course Registry and, more specifically, the possible use of the data through its API at DH2020 is envisioned to attract an increase of attention and participation by colleagues from beyond the European DH research and teaching community and constitutes an effort towards a global communication on DH pedagogy.
