Explore Plato’s Cave in Virtual Reality

XML

1. Abstract

This lightning talk will introduce Escape Plato’s Cave, an interactive virtual reality experience that transports the user into Plato’s famous allegory about the power and peril of education and discuss it as an example of the potential uses of VR for both multimodal pedagogy and public philosophy.

Julia DeWall Uhr (juuh6005@colorado.edu), University of Colorado, United States of America
