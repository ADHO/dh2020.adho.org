DIALOGUES ON DETENTION - AN ITERATIVE, INCLUSIVE APPROACH TO 3D VISUALISATIONS OF COLONIAL CAMPS IN KENYA

XML

1. Abstract

This paper explores the use of 3D visualisations and new media in increasing participation and engagement with colonial history in Kenya. Lying at the intersection of digital technologies, silenced histories and colonial legacy, the study explore ways of creating iterative, community centered approaches to 3D digital reconstructions.
