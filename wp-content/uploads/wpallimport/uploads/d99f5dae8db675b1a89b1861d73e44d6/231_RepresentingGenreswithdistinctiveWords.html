Representing Genres with distinctive Words

XML

1. Abstract

We use Burrows Zeta, Semantic Zeta, LDA, Recursive Feature Elimination and Attention Networks to generate lists of distinctive Words for literary genres in pulp fiction and evaluate their quality with a reader survey and document clustering.
