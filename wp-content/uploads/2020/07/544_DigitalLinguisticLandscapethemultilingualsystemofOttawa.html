Digital Linguistic Landscape:  the multilingual system of Ottawa

XML

1. Abstract

The proposal aims at investigating the digital linguistic landscape of Ottawa, analyzing social network geolocated data, to understand the structure of the urban linguistic system (in terms of variety, use, typology), with a focus on minority languages.

Herbert Natta (herbert.natta@gmail.com), Independent researcher and Maria Valese (m.valese@gmail.com), TU Delft
