Who Teaches When We Teach DH? Some Answers and More Questions.

XML

1. Abstract

At the 2019 Digital Humanities Conference in Utrecht, the authors launched a survey designed to answer the question “Who Teaches When We Teach DH?” In this short talk, we will present and discuss some of the findings that may lead to a better understanding of who teaches and how DH is taught.

Brian Croxall (brian.croxall@byu.edu), Brigham Young University, United States of America and Diane K. Jakacki (dkj004@bucknell.edu), Bucknell University, United States of America
